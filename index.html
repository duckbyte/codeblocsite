Code Bloc

What

Code Bloc is a totally free network of current and aspiring BIPOC developers. Our focus is on individuals who are members of populations that are underrepresented in the tech industry.

Join this group to:

  • Learn to code for free (no experience required!)
  • Network with other BIPOC current/aspiring/former tech workers
  • Participate in skillshares

When

We meet every Wednesday evening from 6pm-8pm, and every Saturday morning from 11am-1pm.

Where

We currently hold meetings at the Rainier Beach Branch of the Seattle Public Library System.

Contact Us

Send us an email at codeblocsea@gmail.com to learn more or join our mailing list!

To respect our mission of empowering underrepresented BIPOC people in tech, we do ask that you only request to join if you are a Black, Indigenous, or other person of color that is underrepresented in the tech industry.
